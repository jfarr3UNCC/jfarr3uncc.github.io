Brooke D'Hondt Portfolio

Project Overview

This project is a portfolio for Brooke D'Hondt, a grapic desinger and video editor. The purpose of the site is to show potentiol clients, employeers, and customers her work. The intended users are anyone who may wish to hire or commision the client or anyone interested in the clients work. The site will contain both personal and professional work done by the client along with contact information to get in touch and a place for the client to advertise different items they are selling such as art prints and clothing.

Wireframe

Site Map

Main Page:

  • Purpose: The landing page of the site
  • Audience: Potentiel Client and Employeers or anyone interested in the clients work
  • Content: Gallery of work with and a Title of the page
  • The page will contain links to all the other sites aswell to links to the clients other pages
  • When the user clicks on the different button on the nav bar they will be taken to the selected page

Contact Page:

  • Purpose: Allow the user to find contact information for the client
  • Audience: Any user interested in contacting the client
  • Content: The contact information for the client
  • The page will contain links to the users social accounts
  • The links will take the user to an external site

Personal Projects:

  • Purpose: The page will show off the personal projects completed by the client
  • Audience: Users interested in the personal work of the client
  • Content: The personal work of the client
  • The page may contain links to external sites or to the sales page

Client Work:

  • Purpose: The page will show off the client/proffesional projects completed by the client<
  • Audience: Users who may be interested in employeeing the client
  • Content: The client/proffesional work of the client
  • The page may contain links to external sites or to the sales page, such as linkedin

Sales:

  • Purpose: The page will show off the items the client is selling
  • Audience: Users who may be interested in purchasing some of the clients work
  • Content: The items the client has for sale
  • The site will link to other sites where the user is selling these items
  • The site will contain buttons that when clicked take the user to external sales sites

Dynamic Functionality

The site will contain the following dynamic items:

  • Photo Gallery: Main Page, Work pages, Sales page
  • interactive images: Works pages, Sales pages
  • The user should be able to see something they are interested in and by clicking it be taken somewhere to find more information about it
  • Forms: Contact page
  • The user should be able to submit some form of contact information that will be sent to the client
