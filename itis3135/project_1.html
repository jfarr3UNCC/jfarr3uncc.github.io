Brooke D'Hondt Portfolio

Project Overview

This project is a portfolio for Brooke D'Hondt, a grapic desinger and video editor. The purpose of the site is to show potentiol clients, employeers, and customers her work. The intended users are anyone who may wish to hire or commision the client or anyone interested in the clients work. The site will contain both personal and professional work done by the client along with contact information to get in touch and a place for the client to advertise different items they are selling such as art prints and clothing.
